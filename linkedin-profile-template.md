## Naming notes

**Indus Sourcing** is chosen to be palatable and trustworthy to U.S. buyers: easy to pronounce (IN-dus) and spell, familiar (the Indus river → India), and it reads as an established B2B firm while still signaling the India focus that *is* your value prop.

- Formal variants: *Indus Sourcing Co.*, *Indus Sourcing Partners*, *Indus Sourcing Group*.
- Other U.S.-friendly options in the same spirit:
  - *Meridian Sourcing* / *Keystone Sourcing* — clean and professional, but no India signal (you'd carry the India story entirely in the tagline).
  - *Bridgeline Sourcing* — leans on the "bridge" idea in plain English.
- **Do before committing:** "Indus" is a fairly common brand word, so check `indussourcing.com` / `.co` availability, run a USPTO TESS trademark search for your class, and confirm the LinkedIn page + social handles are free. If Indus is taken in your space, fall back to Meridian/Keystone/Bridgeline.
