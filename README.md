# India → USA Electronics Export

A personal knowledge base and working document repository for building an electronics export business from India to the United States.

---

## What This Is

This repo tracks my research, planning, and execution as I explore transitioning from an IT career into cross-border electronics trade. The focus is specifically on sourcing electronics from Indian manufacturers and selling them in the U.S. market — primarily via Amazon FBA, with a longer-term play into private label and B2B wholesale.

This is not a theoretical exercise. The goal is a real, operating business.

---

## Project Goals

- **Validate** a viable product category in the U.S. electronics market that Indian manufacturers can competitively supply
- **Source** a compliant, export-ready supplier through IndiaMart and direct outreach to Indian manufacturing clusters
- **Launch** a first SKU on Amazon FBA within 90 days of committing to a product
- **Prove unit economics** — target 20%+ net margin after all landed costs, fees, and PPC
- **Build a brand** around winning products once initial SKUs are validated
- **Transition** from IT career to full-time operator once the business hits $15K+/month in consistent revenue

---

## Guiding Principles

- **Compliance first.** No shortcuts on FCC, UL/ETL, or CPSC. One seizure or account suspension can kill the business before it starts.
- **India's non-Section-301 status is the structural edge.** Use it. Avoid categories where Chinese sellers have unbeatable commodity lock-in.
- **Start small, learn fast.** First order is a market research investment, not a profit center.
- **Side business until the data says otherwise.** IT income funds the experiment without debt pressure.
